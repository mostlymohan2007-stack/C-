# 🌐 HOST YOUR SPACE BATTLE GAME ONLINE

## **Option 1: GitHub Pages (FREE & EASIEST)**

### **Step 1: Create GitHub Repository**
1. Go to [github.com](https://github.com) and create account
2. Click "New repository"
3. Name: `space-battle-game`
4. Make it Public
5. Click "Create repository"

### **Step 2: Upload Your Game Files**
1. Click "Add file" → "Upload files"
2. Upload these files:
   - `index.html` (main page)
   - `play_console.html` (console version)
   - `SpaceBattleGame.exe` (desktop version)
   - `online_version.cpp` (source code)
   - `USER_UPLOAD_GUIDE.txt` (instructions)
   - All your `.dll` files

### **Step 3: Enable GitHub Pages**
1. Go to Settings → Pages
2. Source: "Deploy from a branch"
3. Branch: `main`
4. Click "Save"
5. Your game is live at: `https://yourusername.github.io/space-battle-game/`

---

## **Option 2: Netlify (FREE & CUSTOM DOMAIN)**

### **Step 1: Sign Up**
1. Go to [netlify.com](https://netlify.com)
2. Sign up for free account

### **Step 2: Drag & Drop**
1. Drag your entire `SpaceBattleGame` folder to Netlify
2. It will upload and deploy automatically
3. Get a URL like: `https://your-game-name.netlify.app`

---

## **Option 3: Replit (PLAY DIRECTLY)**

### **Step 1: Create Replit**
1. Go to [replit.com](https://replit.com)
2. Click "+ Create Repl"
3. Choose "C++"

### **Step 2: Add Game Code**
1. Copy all code from `online_version.cpp`
2. Paste into Replit editor
3. Click "Run"

### **Step 3: Share**
1. Click "Share"
2. Copy the link
3. Anyone can play your game online!

---

## **Option 4: Glitch (FREE & EASY)**

### **Step 1: Create Glitch Project**
1. Go to [glitch.com](https://glitch.com)
2. Click "New Project"
3. Choose "hello-express"

### **Step 2: Replace with Your Game**
1. Replace `index.html` with your `index.html`
2. Upload your game files
3. Your game is live instantly!
